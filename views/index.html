Instructions

Welcome to the sunflower field. We have 3 different sunflower songs sung by your favorite artists! Song will being playing after 'OK' is presssed. To pull up this menu again press the "H" key

Song Functions

  • Play: Plays song
  • Pause: Pauses song
  • Volume: Decreases volume
  • Loop: Plays song again after its ended
  • Mute: Mutes song

Colors

Select the buttons to change the color of the visualizer
