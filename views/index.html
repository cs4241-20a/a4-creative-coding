This is a simple 3d snake game.
You can change the direction of the snake by sliding your mouse (drag for some distance).

For Example, if you slide your mouse from the bottom to somewhere middle, then you can let you snake move in up direction
Click "Play" to start playing.
There will be a small menu at the right, which allows you to adjust some config by your own preferences
1. PlayerSpeed : Max 200, the higher the value, the slower the pace
2. PointerCheckThreashold: Used to adjust how long you need to slide in order to change direction (prevent misclicking)
3, 4. Start/Stop Animation: Used to pause and restart
