Welcome to SLPaint!

To change your colors, click on the boxes at the bottom of the canvas. To increase or decrease font size, click on the stroke options at the bottom left of the canvas. To erase, click the pink box at the bottom right. To clear the canvas, click the symbol at the top right of the canvas. To switch between tools, click the dropdown at the top left and select either line, rectangle, or chalk.

Make sure your JavaScript is enabled.This web application is currently not supported with your browser. Please use an alternate browser or download a supported browser.
Supported browsers: Google Chrome, Opera, Firefox, Safari,
and Konqueror.
